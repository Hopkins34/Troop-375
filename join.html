Join the Troop

Scouting is where adults make profound differences in young people's lives. Parents and adult leaders within our youth organization must understand that their role is to create a safe environment where young people can grow, explore, learn to fail, get up, and try again!

Youth

Learn how to join our Boy Scout troop and start your adventure today! This section provides step-by-step guidance on membership requirements, the application process, and what to expect as a new scout.

  • Complete the BSA Youth Membership Application: Here
  • Fill out Medical Forms. These cover a one year period, and updates must be provided as they expire
  • Troop 375 will also gather emergency contact, communication, and transportation information
  • Contact Mr. Panstingle or attend a troop meeting
